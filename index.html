Verbs

tell what the subject does or what the subject is. lets us know what happens. Hint* ask yourself what happened?

Verb Infinitive

is, was, has beent, etc.

Auxillary Verbs aka helping verbs

work to form various tenses for example have be will(shall) and do

Adjectives

words that modify or describe nouns. They come before the word they modify. It can answer the question Which One? What kind Of? How many?

Adverbs

words that modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. they often tell how, when where, or to what degree.
